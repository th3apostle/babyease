Refund Policy

This Refund Policy explains the general approach for returns, refund requests, damaged items, incorrect orders, and customer support.

Return Requests

If you need help with a return, please contact BabyEase with your order number, product name, and a clear explanation of the issue.

Damaged Or Incorrect Items

If an item arrives damaged, incorrect, or incomplete, contact BabyEase as soon as possible with order details and photos if available. This helps review the issue clearly.

Refund Review

Refund requests may require order verification, product details, delivery information, and review of the issue. Approval depends on the order status, item condition, and applicable store process.

Non-Returnable Situations

Some items may not be eligible for return after use, damage caused after delivery, missing packaging, hygiene-related concerns, or requests made outside the allowed return window.

Refund Timing

If a refund is approved, processing time may vary depending on the payment provider, bank, or checkout method used for the order.

Questions

For refund or return questions, please use the Contact Us page and include your order number.
